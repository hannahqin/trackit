trackit.

Staying on track for graduation
has never been easier.

Trackit is an easily digestible way for CS-LSA students at the University of Michigan to view their progress towards graduation and plan out their remaining semesters of college accordingly.

OVERVIEW

Check out an overview of the requirements you have not fulfilled yet without all of the unnecessary details you don't care about.

DETAILS

Take a closer look at the credits you have and haven't completed, and how they contribute towards your CS-LSA degree.

PLANNER

View a calendar of your past semesters and add courses to future semesters to plan out your remaining years at the University of Michigan.

GET STARTED

Get started simply by uploading your advisement checklist from Wolverine Access so we can read the information for you and display it in a more easily comprehensible way.

OVERVIEW PAGE

Easily view your overall progress toward graduation in terms of total credits and core classes.

DETAILS PAGE

View your completed and in-progress requirements for your college, area distribution, and major.

SCHEDULE PLANNER

Plan out the rest of your semesters in a calender view with classes you want to take to complete your remaining requirements.
